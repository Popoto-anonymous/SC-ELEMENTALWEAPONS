ELEMENTAL WEAPONS

Adds elemental weapons for each character

Light and Dark are buyable in Fletz for 210 gold, they resell for the same

Wind and Earth are buyable in Donau for 210 gold, they resell for the same

Fire, Water, and Thunder buyable in Fueno for 250 gold, they resell for the same

Ulara sells the full suite of weapons with more late-game attack values, made for getting you to the moon. for 400G each, and they resell for the same 

Thief Cape is purchasable in Lohan, Thief Scarf is purchasable in Fletz accessory shop, Wingly Mantle in the Wingly Forest

THIEF CAPE, 2 in most stats, 5 speed, 5 to hit rates, prevents Poison, Stun, Arm Block, 1% HP and 10 MP regen (500G)

THIEF SCARF, 5 in most stats, 10 speed, 10 to hit rates, prevents Poison, Stun, Arm Block, 4% HP and 20 MP regen (1000G)

WINGLY MANTLE, 10 in most stats, 15 speed, 15 to hit rates, prevents Poison, Stun, Arm Block, 8% HP and 40 MP regen (1500G)

All weapons are balanced around base game weapons. Except Roses Ulara weapons, they are stronger than the Gladius because you get a free Gladius in the Desert if you know where to look.
